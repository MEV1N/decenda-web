Gravenfall Clinic – Archived Records

During the investigation of the abandoned clinic in Gravenfall, a damaged archive file was recovered from an old workstation used for patient record management.

Most of the system files were corrupted, but one archive remained intact. The contents appear heavily encoded and encrypted. Initial inspection shows no readable information.

Investigators found a small handwritten note near the workstation:

"The records hidden in it."

Further examination of the clinic system logs revealed a recurring email address associated with the archive process:

[jefferson_jologs@yahoo.com](mailto:jefferson_jologs@yahoo.com)

It is believed that this email may have been used as a key or reference when the records were archived.

Your task is to analyze the recovered file and uncover the hidden message inside the clinic archive.

clinic_records.dat
